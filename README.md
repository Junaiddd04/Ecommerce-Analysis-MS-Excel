# 🛒E-Commerce Delivery & Customer Experience Dashboard <br>
📌 Project Overview <br>
This project features a comprehensive Excel-based Analytics Dashboard designed to evaluate operational efficiency and consumer behavior across major quick-commerce platforms (Blinkit, JioMart, and Swiggy Instamart).

The dashboard transforms raw e-commerce data into actionable insights, focusing on delivery performance, revenue distribution, and customer satisfaction metrics to help stakeholders optimize logistics and product offerings.

📊 Key Insights & Analytics <br>
🚚 Operational Performance <br>
On-Time Delivery (OTD): Maintained a strong 86.33% OTD rate, critical for customer retention in quick-commerce.

Average Delivery Time: Performance is tracked at a granular level (Avg. 29.54 mins) to monitor logistics efficiency.

Refund Management: Analysis shows a 45.82% refund request rate, with requests distributed almost equally (~33-34%) across all three platforms.

💰 Revenue & Sales Trends <br>
Top Revenue Drivers: Personal Care ($17.40M) and Grocery ($14.19M) are the primary revenue contributors.

Order Volume vs. Value: While Dairy leads in total order count (16,857 orders), Personal Care boasts the highest Average Order Value (AOV) at $1,052.17.

Platform Preference: Swiggy Instamart emerged as the preferred platform for high-value Personal Care purchases.

⭐ Customer Experience <br>
Satisfaction Score: The current average satisfaction score stands at 3.24/5, indicating a clear opportunity for service quality improvements.

🛠️ Tools & Features Used <br>
Advanced Charts: Utilized Bar, Column, Pie, and Area charts for multi-dimensional data visualization.

Interactive Slicers: Enabled dynamic filtering by Product Category and Platform for customized views.

KPI Cards: High-level summary metrics for immediate executive insights.

Navigation & UX: Integrated links to secondary pages for category-specific deep dives and a seamless user experience.

Data Modeling: Structured raw data to calculate AOV, Refund Rates, and Delivery Performance.

📂 Dashboard Structure <br>
Main Dashboard: The central hub for high-level KPIs and cross-category comparisons.

Category Analysis: Deep dive into specific segments like Beverages, Dairy, and Personal Care.

Platform Analysis: Comparative study of Blinkit, JioMart, and Swiggy Instamart.
